Introduction

The package siland aims to estimate the scale and th shape of influence for landscape variables on measurements. For this two methods are available.The first one is based on buffer computing around location of obserevd data. Landscape descritpion is based on sig files with sf objects. The second one is based on Spatial Influence Functions (SIF). Landscape descritpion is based on raster discretisation for the landscape. If a sig files is given for argument in this secodn fucntion, then a resterisation procedure is achieved. The SIF fucntion describes how the influence of a pixel in the landscape in the raster decreases with the distance from the locations of observed data.
